BallPit Calculator

A calculator to find out just how many playpen balls can fit into a room. Inspired by the XKCD comic.

Have you ever wanted to find out just how many playpen balls will fit in a room? This app helps you find out! Simply input your rooms area, and the depth to which you want to fill it and you can find out precisly how many balls you need. Available now for free without ads on the Play Store.
